LAKERS

Dennis Schröder (d. 15 September 1993), a German professional basketball player from the National Basketball Association (NBA) team to give the Los Angeles Lakers jersey. The 1.85 meters tall player is serving in the point guard position. Before the NBA, Schröder wore the Phantoms Braunschweig and SG Braunschweig jerseys.

On 25 July 2018, Schröder was traded to the Oklahoma City Thunder in a three-team deal involving the Hawks and the Philadelphia 76ers.

On November 18, 2020, Schröder was traded to the Los Angeles Lakers in exchange for Danny Green and the draft rights to first-round pick Jaden McDaniels.
